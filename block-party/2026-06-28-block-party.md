# The Block Party

Late June. 4pm to 8pm. Morada Place.

Paella on the grill. Music in the air. Kids on the street. Neighbors who've never met sitting together for the first time. The whole block, out front, together.

Bring a dish. Bring a chair. Bring your family. That's it.

This is what we've been planning. This is what it's for.
